Jeppiaar Engineering College


Jeppiaar Engineering College is a self-financed institute founded in the year 2001. It is approved by AICTE and is affiliated to Anna University. The college offers B.E, B.Tech at the UG level, ME, M.Tech, MBA, MCA at the PG level. It has the best academic facilities & world-class sports facilities. Well-reputed companies hire productive students from this college with satisfactory salary packages.The training & placement department prepares the students for the corporate world and makes them industry-ready. A lot of campus recruitment drives are organized every year for both internships and placements.
